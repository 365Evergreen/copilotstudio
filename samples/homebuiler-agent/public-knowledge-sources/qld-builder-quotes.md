## Purpose

Provide a clear explanation of how builder quotes are structured in Queensland, what to look for, and how to compare quotes accurately to avoid unexpected variations.

---

## What a Builder Quote Includes

A builder quote typically contains:

- Base house price  
- Standard inclusions  
- Site cost allowances  
- Provisional Sums (PS)  
- Prime Cost (PC) items  
- Facade options  
- Structural upgrades  
- Promotional packages  

Quotes vary widely between builders due to different assumptions and inclusions.

---

## Key Components of a Builder Quote

### Base Price

The base price usually includes:

- Standard floor plan  
- Standard façade  
- Basic inclusions  
- Standard ceiling height  
- Basic electrical layout  

Base prices often exclude:

- Site costs  
- Driveways  
- Flooring (in some cases)  
- Landscaping  
- Fencing  

---

### Standard Inclusions

These vary significantly between builders.  
Common inclusions:

- Basic tiles and carpet  
- Laminate benchtops  
- Standard tapware  
- Basic appliances  
- Standard electrical points  

Higher-end builders may include:

- Stone benchtops  
- Higher ceilings  
- LED lighting  
- Better insulation  

---

### Site Costs

Site costs are one of the biggest variables.  
They depend on:

- Soil type  
- Slope  
- Fill depth  
- Drainage  
- Retaining  
- Service locations  

Public source: <https://qldglobe.information.qld.gov.au/>

---

### Provisional Sums (PS)

PS items are estimates for unknown work such as:

- Earthworks  
- Retaining  
- Drainage  
- Piering  

PS items can lead to variations if underestimated.

---

### Prime Cost (PC) Items

PC items are allowances for materials not yet selected, such as:

- Tiles  
- Tapware  
- Appliances  
- Vanities  

If the owner selects higher-priced items, costs increase.

---

### Promotions and Packages

Builders often offer:

- Free upgrades  
- Appliance packages  
- Facade upgrades  
- Flooring promotions  

These can add value but may have conditions.

---

## How to Compare Builder Quotes

### Compare Like-for-Like

Ensure quotes include:

- Same façade  
- Same ceiling height  
- Same flooring  
- Same electrical layout  
- Same site cost assumptions  

---

### Review PS and PC Items

Look for:

- Unrealistically low allowances  
- Missing PS items for known risks  
- PC allowances below retail prices  

---

### Check Inclusions Lists

Builders may include:

- Different insulation levels  
- Different window types  
- Different slab specifications  
- Different electrical allowances  

---

### Ask for Unit Rates

Examples:

- $/m³ for excavation  
- $/m for drainage  
- $/m² for retaining  
- $/m for piering  

This helps compare quotes accurately.

---

## Red Flags in Builder Quotes

- Very low site cost allowances  
- Missing PS items  
- PC allowances below realistic prices  
- Excessive exclusions  
- No soil test before contract  
- No engineering assumptions provided  

These often lead to variations later.
